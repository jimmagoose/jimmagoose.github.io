Alex Whelan

I'm a PhD student enrolled in the Centre for Doctoral Training in Biomedical Artificial Intelligence at The University of Edinburgh.

My research background is in Neurorobotics, which is a field that sits at the interface of Neuroscience, Robotics and AI. I'm currently working towards a PhD in Computational Psychiatry in the lab of Dr. Peggy Seriès.

I have an MPhil in Neurorobotics (University of Sheffield, 2018-2020), MSc in Computational Intelligence and Robotics (University of Sheffield, 2016-2017) and a BEng in Mechanical Engineering (Sheffield Hallam University, 2012-2016).

My other passions include advocating for mental health (I'm open to events in which I could help, so get in touch!), running, and currently learning Spanish. I took a year out from formal studies in 2018 to travel Mexico and learn Spanish, and am looking to travel back to Latin America soon!
